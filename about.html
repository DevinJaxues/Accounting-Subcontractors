Meet the Team Behind the Numbers

While working closely with general contractors and subcontractors, we saw a recurring challenge: many owners didn’t have clear visibility into what they were truly making on each job. Job costing, cash flow forecasting, and real-time financial insights were often missing — leaving contractors in the dark.

That gap inspired us to branch off and build a dedicated solution for the construction industry. With deep experience serving GCs and subcontractors, we designed Accounting Subs to bridge the disconnect between project management and accounting. Our systems integrate JobTread and QuickBooks Online, backed by CPA oversight, so contractors not only stay compliant but also gain the clarity they need to grow profitably.
